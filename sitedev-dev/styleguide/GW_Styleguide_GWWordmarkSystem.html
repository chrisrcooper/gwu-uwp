GW Schools and Colleges

All GW schools and colleges are to use the GW wordmarks on their publications and communications. Wordmarks have been developed for each school and college that complement the GW wordmarks. Contact GW Graphic Design for details regarding the specific rules governing their use and to obtain electronic files.

GW Offices and Departments

Administrative units are to use University wordmarks as the primary identification on all publications. No other logos are permitted.
