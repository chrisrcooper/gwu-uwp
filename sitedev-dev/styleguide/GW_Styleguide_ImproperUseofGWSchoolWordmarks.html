Improper Use of the GW School Wordmarks

Print Resources

The fundamental rules and regulations governing proper use of the GW wordmarks listed under “GW Wordmark System” and “Improper Uses of GW Wordmarks” apply to the school wordmarks as well.

In brief, school wordmarks may not be reconfigured, recreated, or altered in any way. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Adding or subtracting typography, icons, or images
  • Applying effects such as drop shadows or highlights
  • Rotating, positioning at an angle, flopping, or scaling out of proportion
  • Containing within a box or shape

To ensure legibility and adequate contrast, wordmarks should only be placed on plain backgrounds and not on patterned areas of other design elements or photographs.
