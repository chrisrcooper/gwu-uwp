VisionThe Communication Standards Guide will be a key tool to ensure that each conversation about GW is consistent, on-message, and allows both parties to leave that conversation having moved closer to accomplishing a task. As such, it will be updated on a regular basis and will always reflect the latest communications standards and goals for how we tell the GW story.

Writing for gwu.edu When writing for gwu.edu, everything you write should put the user first. This is key to successful communication, and means that you must pay close attention to not only what is said, but also how it is said. Remember, the easier it is for users to find and act on information on gwu.edu, the more successful the site will be.

GW Global Brand

The GW Brand is the story we want to tell. We want to tell our story consistently, no matter how we are communicating with our external and internal audiences. The brand guidelines outline how we want people to think of GW, whether they are hearing about us online, in print, through the written word, through speech, or visually.
